This class project was centered around the idea of visual storytelling by showing the progression of something over time. I chose to show the general history of BMW motorcycles, starting in 1923 when their first motorcycle was released and ending in 1993, which marked one of their last major designs innovations before the turn of the century. Each of these cards feature vehicle specifications and the significance of the model, each chosen for their significance to the history of BMW.
