This class project had me choose a childhood memory and represent it without any images, relying instead on typography and color to illustrate the subject. My chosen memory was all of the time I spent folding origami paper when I was younger, and I brought this into the design using the white lines in the background, which are the lines left on paper once unfolded from the shape of an origami crane. The text is the lyrics to a song which invoked similar emotions to the chosen memory.
