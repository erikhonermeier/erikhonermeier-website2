This class project charged myself and other students with creating a fictional activist organization and creating a series of campaign posters to promote its cause. Our chosen cause was access to equitable education, particularly for low-income schools. I designed my campaign around the unfortunate fact that while the American Constitution grants the rights of many things, it doesn't guarantee a fair education for anyone, and promoted the idea of educational equity from that angle.
